## The BioInitiative Report

## Protecting Yourself

While you can't eliminate EMF exposure in the modern world, you can reduce it:

**Distance is your friend.** Radiation follows the inverse square law—double the distance, quarter the exposure. Keep phones away from your body. Don't sleep with devices near your bed.

**Wired connections.** Use ethernet cables instead of WiFi when possible. Connect computers directly rather than using wireless peripherals.

**Airplane mode.** Put phones in airplane mode at night and when not in use. This disables the most intense emissions.

**EMF shielding.** Specialized fabrics, paints, and films can block electromagnetic fields. Consider shielding sleeping areas.

**Advocate.** Support local efforts to stop 5G deployment until proper safety studies are conducted. The telecommunications industry has billions in lobbying power—citizens need to organize.
